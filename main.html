About
Ribhu Sanyal

Ribhu Sanyal is a devoted practitioner and teacher of Dhrupad, the most ancient and meditative form of Indian classical vocal music. Trained in the revered Dagar tradition, he brings decades of rigorous sādhanā to every lesson — guiding students through the profound depths of Nād (sound), Rāga (melody), and Tāl (rhythm). Based in India, he teaches students across the globe through live online sessions.

His teaching is rooted in authentic lineage transmission and emphasises patient, methodical practice, inner listening, and the spiritual dimension of this sacred art form.
